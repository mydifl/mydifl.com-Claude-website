# DIFL – Dante Institute of Foreign Languages

## Where Languages Take Flight

**DIFL – Dante Institute of Foreign Languages** is a Jaipur-based foreign language institute with a legacy of over **55 years** in language education.

For five decades, DIFL has trained generations — your grandparents, your parents, you, and now your children.

We teach languages not merely as subjects, but as living skills for communication, careers, travel, study abroad, migration, business, culture, and confidence.

## Our Teaching Philosophy

### TTP – Thought Translation Process

At DIFL, we believe language learning is not just about translating words.

It is about transforming the learner’s **Thought Translation Process**.

Our unique TTP-based pedagogy helps students understand a foreign language through their own mother tongue, gradually training them to think, structure, and express naturally in the target language.
